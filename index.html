Welcome!

This is a Choose Your Own Adventure game. If you have not heard of this type of story before, it is essentially an interactive book. The main character of the book is you!! As you read through the story, you will be given choices to make. Each choice directs you to another page (not necessarily in order!) and changes the path the story takes.

You will want a place to take notes, to keep track of things such as your health, inventory, and important notes. Like the physical books, this story will *not* do so for you.

There are many choices that will ultimately lead to your demise - fear not! You can always start the story over and try again! There is only one "good" ending. Will you find it?

New chapters will be released periodically - keep an eye on this page for the latest updates!
