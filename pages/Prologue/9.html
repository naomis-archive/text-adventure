You know you cannot afford to be late to work again, and you do not know the first thing about fixing cars. So calling a cab is the only option. You pull out your phone and punch the number for the cab company in on the keypad - you really need to get around to buying a proper smart phone one of these days. A few minutes later, the driver pulls up to take you to work.

Your thoughts wander with no direction as you make the thirty minute trip to the office. Staring out the window, you are completely distracted - for some reason, today just feels different. The sight of your towering office building pulls you back in to reality. The cab driver pulls up to the main entrance and stops to let you out. Giving him the little bit of cash you had in your wallet, you thank him for the ride and walk toward the building.

Just out of the corner of your eye, you see a strange sparkle. What was that? It looks like it came from the courtyard garden? It could have been a trick of the eye, the sun hitting something in just the right way. But with the way today has been going, it might be worth looking at more closely.
