"Can you take me to that ramen place on 3rd?" you ask the driver. Looking at you in the rear view mirror, he silently nods. You go back to looking out the window, still fixated on the courtyard, as the driver makes a turn to head to the restaurant.

Your daydreams are interrupted when the driver screams. "OH SH-" You look up just in time to see the other car. There is no way the driver could react in time; the car plows into your cab and sends it flying through the air. The view out the window is spinning, and you lose all sense of direction. When the cab crashes back down, it lands on its roof - the roof collapses, crushing you. Your last thoughts before you fade away were still focused on that sparkle in the courtyard...

You died. Try again?

Start over?
