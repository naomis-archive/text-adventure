You know you must hurry to get to the courtyard! You pull out your phone to call a cab, when someone shouts your name.

Looking up, you see your neighbour Lucas.

"Hey, you heading somewhere?" he asks.

You explain that you are heading to the office, and he tells you he also has some work to do. Lucas offers you a ride, which you gratefully accept, and the two of you make your way to the office building.

When you arrive, you step out of the car and head toward the main entrance. The sparkle in the courtyard is still there, and it catches your eye quickly under the night sky. Lucas does not seem to even notice it.

"You coming?" He is standing there impatiently, waiting for your response. You tell him to go on in, and let him know you'll be right behind him. He gives you a strange look before taking off.

You walk down the gravel path - the courtyard has a very different environment at night. You finally reach the back corner, where you first noticed the strange light come from. Looking around, you do not notice anything unusual - until you spot a bright blue crystal on the ground. You pick it up, looking it over. It appears to give off a bright light.
