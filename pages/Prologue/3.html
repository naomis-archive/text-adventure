You know you cannot afford the rather exorbitant cost of calling a cab, so you decide to try your hand at fixing it yourself. First, though, you give your boss a call to let her know the situation. You pull out your phone and punch her number in on the keypad - you really need to get around to buying a proper smart phone one of these days.

RING RING RING - The sound of the line ringing on the other end is jarring. RING RING RING - Strange, usually your boss is quick to answer her phone. A couple more rings and you end up reaching her voicemail box - you leave a message explaining the situation. Hopefully you do not land yourself in hot water, but what other option do you have?

After putting your phone away, you roll up your sleeves and prepare for this. You pop the hood of the car and take a look at the engine - staring at it for a few minutes, you realise you have absolutely no clue what you are looking at. Why did you think this was a good idea?

BUZZ BUZZ! The vibration of your phone in your pocket interrupts your train of thought. You pull out the phone - the boss is calling you back. Of course you answer; though you quickly regret it. A few minutes of yelling and frustration (from your boss - you could never work up the courage to yell back) and you are now out of a job. Closing the hood of your car, you head back inside - an increasingly intense sinking feeling building in your chest. You have no idea what you will do now - without your job, you'll end up losing everything...
